Who We Are

POWERAMA SOLUTIONS

A leading integrated Engineering Services and IoT Product development company operating in Pakistan and the Middle East. Our expertise lies in delivering client-specific solutions for projects encompassing MEP, Renewable Energy, IoT, and the Embedded industry. From engineering design to procurement and product development, we offer a comprehensive package of services

As a multinational company, we have earned a reputation in the region for our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and on-time project delivery. With all the necessary faculties in-house, we are fully equipped to handle turnkey projects efficiently. This unique strength enables us to serve as a reliable MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing Design) services provider to a diverse range of international clients.

Join us on our journey of innovation and excellence as we continue to serve our clients with world-class engineering services and IoT product solutions.
